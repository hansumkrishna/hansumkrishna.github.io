Speakers

Mohan Sundaram

Startup Mentor

With a keen interest on employing innovative ideas for disability alleviation, he is engaged in activism for the disabled and has also done impressive volunteering work, fueling the desires of the disabled to take-off into the world of possibilities.



Mohan Sundaram presently mentors startups at NSSRCEL and has worked across a wide range of industries in top management and business leadership roles.

Chetan Maini

Entrepreneur & Technologist

Being a motor-lover made him build remote-controlled cars and planes at the tender age of 10 and go-carts as a teenager. He has been working with electric vehicles for over 20 years and has built India's first electric car, and also holds over 40 patents in battery and energy management systems in EV technologies.


Chetan Maini is an Indian business industrialist who is best known as the founder and advisor of Mahindra Reva Electric Vehicle Co Ltd. In the past, he has served as the Chief Executive Officer, Chief of Technology and Strategy officer, and Deputy chairman at Mahindra Reva Electric car Co Ltd.

Priya Varadarajan

Founder of DURGA

A chartered accountant by profession, a DURGA by choice, Priya Varadarajan, is the founder of DURGA, “an initiative that brings out inherent skills among Indian women and young girls to recognise and deter crime”.


The Nirbhaya Rape Case was an eye opener to many across the country and Priya felt that something must be done with skill building in Indian women; Hence, DURGA was born.She is globally recognised as a champion of women safety and has also received the “Outstanding Achievement Award” at the Asian Voice Charity Awards in Britain.

Mind of an engineer and skill in business is one of the deadliest combinations on the route to success.He has pioneered a strong Intellectual Property Portfolio and is an inspiration to MBA grads being a visiting faculty at many management schools.

About TEDxBITBANGALORE

The TEDx program is designed to help communities, organizations and individuals to spark conversations and communicate thoughts and theories. It is a platform that brings forth the brilliance of TED and lets us indulge in the spirit of “ideas worth spreading”
At TEDx, a profusion of events such as screening of TED talks, videos and live presenters are brought together to simulate the TED environment on a local scale. TEDx events are planned and coordinated independently under a license granted by TED. Great ideas are not born in conference rooms and global meets, but in the back benches of classrooms, in college corridors and such. TEDx BIT was an initiative to mold such ideas and transcend to greater heights.
We have previously organized, with the theme “I Believe” live presenter event and “The Big picture” screening that comprised of a diversity of ideas which served to amaze, inspire and energize our audience!
